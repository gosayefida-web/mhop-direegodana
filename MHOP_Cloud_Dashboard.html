MHOP Protocol reference
Clinical pathway across the glycaemic continuum
Stage A — Prediabetes / NDH (HbA1c 42–47 mmol/mol)
Entry criteriaHbA1c 42–47 mmol/mol · preserved insulin secretion · no prior diabetes diagnosis
TIR target≥ 70%
Primary goalRemission (HbA1c < 42) or stabilisation
Escalation triggerHbA1c exceeds 48 mmol/mol or progressive deterioration despite optimisation
Stage B — Established T2DM (HbA1c > 48 mmol/mol)
Entry criteriaConfirmed T2DM · progressive insulin resistance · declining β-cell reserve
TIR target≥ 70%
Primary goalControlled T2DM · prevent multisystem deterioration
Escalation triggerPersistent poor control · declining renal / BP / weight / foot health · advanced β-cell failure
Stage C — Advanced T2DM with β-cell failure
Entry criteriaLong-standing T2DM · severe β-cell failure · insulin dependence · high disease burden
Key markersC-peptide · HOMA-β · glycaemic variability · insulin requirements
Primary goalRestoration of endogenous insulin secretion · reduced insulin dependence · stabilisation
Advanced therapyCell-based and regenerative approaches after exhaustion of conventional strategies
CGM targets — international consensus
TIR (70–180 mg/dL)≥ 70%
TAR Level 1 (>180 mg/dL)< 25%
TBR Level 1 (<70 mg/dL)< 4%
TBR Level 2 (<54 mg/dL)< 1%
